Built solo by a Filipino student, para sa lahat ng Pilipino.

PH StudentKit was built in 2026 by Charlie James Abejo, a Computer Science student frustrated by hopping between 20+ websites just to finish a single capstone project. So he built one site with everything. No signup. No paywall. No catches.

Why this exists

Filipino students juggle dozens of tools: one for citations, one for AI help, one for scholarships info, one for resume building, one for free Office tutorials, one for thesis writing tips. Every tool wants a signup. Many cost money.

This shouldn't be that hard. So I built one site that has everything I needed during my own capstone — and made it free for every Filipino student who comes after me.

Privacy

What we collect: Anonymous usage data (page visits, browser type) via Vercel Analytics for improvement. No personal data, no cookies, no tracking pixels.

What's stored on your device: Resume drafts, portfolio builder state, capstone tracker progress, theme preference (light/dark). All in localStorage on YOUR browser only. Clear browser data → it's gone.

AI conversations: Your prompts go to free AI providers (Google Gemini, Pollinations.ai, HackClub AI) which have their own privacy policies. We don't store or log them on our side.

Third-party APIs: Weather data via Open-Meteo, currency via open.er-api.com — see their respective policies.
